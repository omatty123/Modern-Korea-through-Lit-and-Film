Week 10: Student Presentations 6/1 – 6/5
Synthesis Across All 13 Films
Which film changed your understanding of Korean history most, and why?
What recurring patterns did you notice in how Korean cinema represents the past?
  • Who gets to be the protagonist? Who is silent?
  • How do films from different decades treat the same historical events?
  • When do films challenge the state narrative, and when do they reinforce it?
How did the literary readings deepen or complicate what the films showed?
What is one question this course left you with?
